We clean carpets and upholstery right across Gloucester, from the city-centre and dockside flats to the family homes of Quedgeley, Hardwicke, Hucclecote, Longlevens and Abbeymead. Gloucester is part of our core service area alongside Cheltenham, so there is no travel surcharge.

## Cleaning matched to the fibre

Gloucester homes run from Victorian terraces with original wool carpets to modern estates with hard-wearing synthetic pile. We identify what you have before we start and choose the method to suit it — a low-moisture clean for wool and natural fibres that over-wetting would damage, and the most effective approach for tougher synthetic carpets. Either way you get fast drying and a genuinely clean result, not a soaked carpet that takes a day to dry.

## Upholstery, stains and commercial work

As well as carpets we clean sofas, armchairs and other upholstery, treat stains with an honest pre-assessment, and take on commercial work for offices, lettings and hospitality across the city. Full-house and contract jobs carry a discount, quoted on request.
