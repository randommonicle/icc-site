Winchcombe was one of Gloucestershire's great medieval wool towns, and its period cottages and Cotswold-stone houses are exactly the kind of homes that suit careful carpet cleaning. We cover Winchcombe and the surrounding GL54 villages as part of our core service area, alongside Cheltenham and Gloucester — so there is no travel surcharge.

## Right for period and stone homes

Older homes around Winchcombe, Greet, Gretton and Sudeley often have wool, Axminster or natural-fibre carpets and rugs, and those are the materials an over-wet clean can shrink or brown. Our low-moisture method is the gentler, safer route: controlled moisture, fibre-appropriate products, and fast drying, so carpets come out genuinely clean without being soaked.
