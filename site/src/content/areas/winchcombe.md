Winchcombe was one of Gloucestershire's great medieval wool towns, and its period cottages and Cotswold-stone houses are exactly the kind of homes that suit careful carpet cleaning. We cover Winchcombe and the surrounding GL54 villages as part of the wider Gloucestershire area we serve — beyond our core Cheltenham and Gloucester patch, so a small out-of-area surcharge applies, always confirmed when you book.

## Right for period and stone homes

Older homes around Winchcombe, Greet, Gretton and Sudeley often have wool, Axminster or natural-fibre carpets and rugs, and those are the materials an over-wet clean can shrink or brown. Our low-moisture method is the gentler, safer route: controlled moisture, fibre-appropriate products, and fast drying, so carpets come out genuinely clean without being soaked.
