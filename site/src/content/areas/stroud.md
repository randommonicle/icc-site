We cover Stroud and the Five Valleys as part of the wider Gloucestershire area we serve. Stroud sits outside our core Cheltenham and Gloucester patch, so a small out-of-area surcharge applies — always confirmed when you book, never a surprise on the day.

## Built for the valleys' period homes

Stroud and the surrounding villages — Nailsworth, Minchinhampton, Painswick, Stonehouse, Bisley and Chalford — are full of stone cottages and period houses, and those often have wool or natural-fibre carpets and rugs. These are exactly the materials that harsh, over-wet cleaning can shrink or brown. Our low-moisture method is the gentler, safer route for them: controlled moisture, fibre-appropriate products, and fast drying.

## Honest, itemised, no surprises

You get a fully itemised quote that includes the out-of-area surcharge up front, so the price you see is the price you pay. We are also straight about results — fresh marks lift well; bleach damage and some old staining are permanent, and we will tell you before we start.
