## At home with Cotswold-stone houses

Cirencester, South Cerney, Fairford, Lechlade and the villages around them have plenty of period and Cotswold-stone homes, and with them wool and natural-fibre carpets and rugs. Those fibres reward a careful, low-moisture clean and can be harmed by an over-wet one. We identify the fibre first and match the method to it, so carpets come out clean and dry quickly rather than soaked.
