About Intelligent Carpet Cleaning

Cheltenham-based carpet specialists combining genuine expertise with modern technology to deliver results that last.

Why we are different

Most carpet cleaning companies treat every floor the same. We do not. Every carpet has a different fibre type, pile construction, and soiling profile. Getting that wrong damages the carpet, sometimes permanently.

We built our AI assistant because we were tired of customers being misled by rental machines and cut-price operators. Our AI explains what your carpet actually needs before anyone steps through your door, so there are no surprises on the day and no damage after the fact.

Based in Cheltenham, we serve the full Gloucestershire area for both residential and commercial clients.
